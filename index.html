🔗 Zero-cost Sanifu integration test
Before any formal partnership, run a manual simulation. On Thursday night after the vendor tallies orders, send the aggregated list to Sanifu's WhatsApp number or demo account:
📤 Send to Sanifu demo account Thursday night:
VENDOR: [Name] · WEEK: [Date] AGGREGATED DEMAND TALLY: • Tomatoes: 23kg • Onions: 15kg • Unga 2kg packs: 18 units • Eggs (trays): 8 trays • Sukuma wiki: 12 bunches REQUEST: Generate procurement recommendation and reorder quantities for next week baseline.
If Sanifu can ingest this and generate a procurement recommendation — you have demonstrated the integration without writing a single line of code. That is your partnership conversation starter.
